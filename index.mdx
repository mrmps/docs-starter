## Why these steps matter

Your OpenClaw agent lives on a Mac Mini. For it to work reliably, two things need to be true:

1. **The Mac Mini is reachable** — Tailscale creates a secure, always-on connection so your agent can be accessed without opening ports or configuring firewalls.
2. **macOS is up to date** — Software updates include security patches and stability fixes that keep your agent running smoothly.

Both steps take under 10 minutes. Let's get started.
