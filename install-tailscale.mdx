# Install Tailscale

Tailscale creates a secure, private network between your Mac Mini and our systems. This is how your OpenClaw agent stays connected — no open ports, no firewall changes, no complicated networking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Tailscale">
    Open **Safari** on your Mac Mini and go to:

    ```
    https://tailscale.com/download/mac
    ```

    Click **Download for macOS** to get the installer.

    <Note>
      You can also install via the Mac App Store — search for "Tailscale" and install the app by Tailscale Inc.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install the app">
    1. Open the downloaded `.zip` file (it should open automatically)
    2. Drag **Tailscale** into your **Applications** folder
    3. Open **Tailscale** from your Applications folder

    macOS may ask you to confirm opening an app downloaded from the internet. Click **Open**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in to Tailscale">
    1. Click the **Tailscale icon** in your menu bar (top-right of the screen)
    2. Click **Log in**
    3. A browser window will open — **sign in with the account we set up for you**

    <Warning>
      Use the Tailscale credentials provided by your SetupClaw team, not a personal account. This ensures your Mac Mini joins the correct network.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the connection">
    Once signed in:

    1. Click the **Tailscale icon** in the menu bar
    2. You should see **Connected** with a green dot
    3. Your machine name will appear in the list

    That's it — your Mac Mini is now securely connected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable Tailscale at login">
    To ensure Tailscale starts automatically when your Mac Mini restarts:

    1. Click the **Tailscale icon** in the menu bar
    2. Click **Preferences**
    3. Check **Start Tailscale when I log in**

    This keeps your agent connected even after a power outage or restart.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see the Tailscale icon in the menu bar">
    Open **Finder → Applications** and double-click **Tailscale** to launch it. The icon should appear in the top-right of your screen.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="It says 'VPN Configuration' permission needed">
    macOS requires you to allow VPN configurations for Tailscale:

    1. Go to **System Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**
    2. Find Tailscale and click **Allow**
    3. Restart Tailscale

    You may need to enter your Mac password.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Connection keeps dropping">
    1. Make sure your Mac Mini is connected to a stable internet connection (Ethernet is best)
    2. Check that **Start Tailscale when I log in** is enabled
    3. Restart your Mac Mini and check if the connection comes back automatically

    If the issue persists, contact us at [mike@setupclaw.com](mailto:mike@setupclaw.com).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
